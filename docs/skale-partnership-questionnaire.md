**4. Briefly describe your project.**

Ultravioleta DAO builds open-source infrastructure for the x402 payment protocol â€” the emerging standard for per-request monetization of APIs and AI agent resources. **6. Why did you choose SKALE over others and what SKALE feature(s) most benefit your project?**

x402 is built for high-frequency, low-value payments â€” the kind of traffic generated by AI agents querying APIs dozens of times per minute. On most chains, gas fees make this economically absurd: a $0.01 API call shouldn't cost $0.05 in gas. SKALE solves this completely.

Three SKALE features are directly load-bearing for Ultravioleta DAO:

- **EIP-3009 support in USDC.e**: SKALE Base's bridged USDC.e supports `transferWithAuthorization`, the ERC-3009 primitive that x402 relies on. This enables the full gasless payment flow where the payer signs an authorization off-chain and the facilitator submits it on-chain â€” no approval transactions, no two-step flows.
- **Throughput and finality**: SKALE's block times and throughput match the latency requirements of agentic workloads. An AI agent can't wait 15 seconds for payment confirmation between API calls.
